Power spectrum-based connection admission control for ATM networks

 Chung-Ju Chang et al

Patent number: 6075770
Filing date: Nov 20, 1996
Issue date: Jun 13, 2000

A method and system are disclosed for constructing a power spectrum based connection admission control table and using such a table in a communications network, such as an ATM network. Power spectrum parameters, such as the power spectrum DC component .gamma., half power bandwidth B.sub.w and average power .PSI..sub.w are transformed to .gamma., an equivalent half power bandwidth B.sub.e and an equivalent average power .PSI..sub.e, where B.sub.e is a predetermined constant. Since B.sub.e is constant, the transformation reduces the memory requirements for constructing a connection admission control table.

Claims

1. A method for constructing a power spectrum based connection admission control table including the steps of:

(a) using a predetermined scheduling policy and a predetermined quality of service for communications, determining a maximum equivalent aggregate average power .PSI..sub.max of an arrival rate of cells for all calls admitted to a node, which maintains said predetermined quality of service, as a function of at least an aggregate power spectrum DC component .gamma..sub.T but not as a function of a constant equivalent half-power bandwidth B.sub.e of said arrival rate of cells for all calls, for which B.sub.e individual component equivalent average powers .PSI..sub.e are determined for all calls,
(b) storing the value of .PSI..sub.max determined in step (a) in a table entry that is indexed by at least the value of .gamma..sub.T used to determined said stored value of .PSI..sub.max, and
(c) repeating steps (a)-(b) for multiple different values of .gamma..sub.T in a range of expected values for .gamma..sub.T.

2. The method of claim 1 further comprising the step of using a queue-least threshold scheduling policy.

3. The method of claim 1 wherein said step (a) further comprises the step of determining said constant B.sub.e as a value less than each expected half power bandwidth value B.sub.w of each call set up request.

4. The method of claim 1 wherein said connection admission control table is used to admit type-1 traffic calls for streamed communications and type-2 traffic calls for transactional communications, wherein received type-1 traffic cells of admitted calls are buffered in said node in a separate queue from received type-2 traffic cells until said admitted cells are routed onto an outgoing link from said node, said method further comprising the steps of:

(d) prior to performing step (a), determining a maximum aggregate cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.max of all type-2 traffic calls that can be accommodated by said node, and
(e) repeating steps (a)-(c) for same multiple values of .gamma..sub.T once for each of multiple different values of an aggregate cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.T for all admitted type-2 traffic calls between 0 and .GAMMA..sub.max,
wherein said step (a) comprises determining .PSI..sub.max as a function of a value of .GAMMA..sub.T for admitted type-2 traffic calls as well as said value of .gamma..sub.T for admitted type-1 traffic calls,
wherein said step (b) comprises storing said value .PSI..sub.max in a table entry that is indexed by said value of .GAMMA..sub.T as well as said value of .gamma..sub.T used to determine said value of .PSI..sub.max in said step (a).

5. A method for admitting calls using a power spectrum based connection admission control table comprising the steps of:

(a) producing power spectrum DC component .gamma., half power bandwidth B.sub.w, and average power .PSI..sub.w parameters of a cell arrival rate of a new call,
(b) transforming the three parameters .gamma., B.sub.w, .PSI..sub.w to two parameters .gamma. and an equivalent average power .PSI..sub.e, for a predetermined constant B.sub.e,
(c) retrieving from a predetermined table, a maximum aggregate equivalent average power .PSI..sub.max that can be accommodated by a node for all admitted calls which .PSI..sub.max is indexed by at least a sum of an aggregate power spectrum DC component .gamma..sub.T for all calls currently admitted at said node and .gamma., and
(d) determining whether or not to admit said new call by comparing a sum of an aggregate average power .PSI..sub.T of all calls currently admitted to said node and .PSI..sub.e to .PSI..sub.max.

6. The method for claim 5 further comprising the steps of:

(e) receiving in a call set up request from a cell source one or more parameters of said cell arrival rate in the time domain, and
(f) transforming the time domain parameters to the frequency domain to produce said parameters .gamma., B.sub.w, .PSI..sub.w.

7. The method of claim 5 wherein said constant B.sub.e is selected so as to be less than each expected value of B.sub.w, said method further comprising the step of:

(e) low pass filtering and integrating .PSI..sub.w to produce .PSI..sub.e.

8. The method of claim 5 further comprising the step of:

in response to admitting a call to said node:
(e) increasing .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T by the values .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of said admitted call, and
(f) storing in a second table entry said values of .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of said admitted call if there is at least one said admitted call.

9. The method of claim 8 further comprising the step of:

in response to disestablishing a call previously admitted to said node:
(g) retrieving said parameters .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of the disestablished call, and
(h) reducing .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T by the retrieved values of .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e from step (g).

10. The method of claim 5 wherein said node admits both type-1 traffic calls for streamed communications and type-2 traffic calls for transactional communications, said method further comprising the steps of:

(e) maintaining an aggregate cell arrival rate parameter .GAMMA..sub.max of all type-2 traffic calls admitted to said node and said parameters .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T for all type-1 traffic calls admitted to said node,
wherein said step (c) further comprises the step of using a value .GAMMA..sub.max as well as the sum of .gamma..sub.T and .gamma. to retrieve .PSI..sub.max for determining whether or not to admit a type-1 traffic call, where said value .GAMMA..sub.max being a maximum aggregate cell arrival rate.

11. The method of claim 10 further comprising the steps of:

admitting a new type-2 traffic call by:
(f) receiving a cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.new for said admitted type-2 traffic call,
(g) retrieving from said predetermined table a value of .PSI..sub.max contained in a table entry indexed by the sum of .GAMMA..sub.T and .GAMMA..sub.new and .gamma..sub.T, and
(h) determining whether or not to admit said new type-2 traffic call by comparing a sum of .GAMMA..sub.T and .GAMMA..sub.new to a maximum aggregate cell arrival rate for type-2 traffic calls .GAMMA..sub.max and by comparing .PSI..sub.T to the value of .PSI..sub.max retrieved in said step (g).

12. An apparatus for constructing a power spectrum based connection admission control table comprising:

a processor for, using a predetermined scheduling policy and a predetermined quality of service for communications, determining a maximum equivalent aggregate average power .PSI..sub.max of an arrival rate of cells for all calls admitted to a node, which maintains said predetermined quality of service, as a function of at least an aggregate power spectrum DC component .gamma..sub.T but not as a function of a constant equivalent half-power bandwidth B.sub.e of said arrival rate of cells for all calls, for which B.sub.e individual component equivalent average powers .PSI..sub.e are determined for all calls, and
a memory for storing each value of .PSI..sub.max determined by said processor in a table entry that is indexed by at least the value of .gamma..sub.T used by said processor to determined said stored value of .PSI..sub.max, and wherein said processor determines a value of .PSI..sub.max for multiple different values of .gamma..sub.T in a range of expected values for .gamma..sub.T.

13. The apparatus of claim 12 wherein said processor uses a queue-least-threshold scheduling policy.

14. The apparatus of claim 12 wherein said processor determines said constant B.sub.e as a value less than each expected half power bandwidth value B.sub.w of each call set up request.

15. The apparatus of claim 12 wherein said table stored in said memory is used to admit type-1 traffic calls for streamed communications and type-2 traffic calls for transactional communications, wherein received type-1 traffic cells of admitted calls are buffered in said node in a separate queue from received type-2 traffic cells until said admitted cells are routed onto an outgoing link from said node,

wherein prior to determining .PSI..sub.max, said processor determines a maximum aggregate cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.max of all type-2 traffic calls that can be accommodated by said node,
wherein said processor determines .PSI..sub.max for the same multiple values of .gamma..sub.T once for each of multiple different values, between 0 and .GAMMA..sub.max, of an aggregate cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.T for all admitted type-2 traffic calls,
wherein said processor determines .PSI..sub.max as a function of a value of .GAMMA..sub.T for admitted type-2 traffic calls as well as said value of .gamma..sub.T for admitted type-1 traffic calls, and
wherein said memory stores said value .PSI..sub.max in a table entry that is indexed by said value of .GAMMA..sub.T as well as said value of .gamma..sub.T used to determine said value of .PSI..sub.max.

16. A node in a communications network comprising:

(a) a first circuit for producing power spectrum DC component .gamma., half power bandwidth B.sub.w, and average power .PSI..sub.w parameters of a cell arrival rate of a new call,
(b) a second circuit for transforming the three parameters .gamma., B.sub.w, .PSI..sub.w to two parameters .gamma. and an equivalent average power .PSI..sub.e, for a predetermined constant B.sub.e,
(c) a predetermined power spectrum based connection admission control table for retrieving a maximum aggregate equivalent average power .PSI..sub.max that can be accommodated by said node for all admitted calls, which .PSI..sub.max is indexed by at least a sum of an aggregate power spectrum DC component .gamma..sub.T for all calls currently admitted at said node and .gamma., and for determining whether or not to admit a new call by comparing a sum of an aggregate average power .PSI..sub.T of all calls currently admitted to said node and .PSI..sub.e to .PSI..sub.max.

17. The apparatus of claim 16 wherein said first circuit receives in a call set up request from a cell source one or more parameters of said cell arrival rate in the time domain, and transforms the time domain parameters to the frequency domain to produce said parameters .gamma., B.sub.w, .PSI..sub.w.

18. The apparatus of claim 16 wherein said constant B.sub.e is selected so as to be less than each expected value of B.sub.w, said second circuit further comprising:

(d) a low pass filter and integrator for low pass filtering and integrating .PSI..sub.w to produce .PSI..sub.e.

19. The apparatus of claim 16 further comprising:

(e) an accumulator for, in response to admitting a call to said node, increasing .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T by the values .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of said admitted call, and
(f) a user information table for storing in an entry thereof said values of .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of said admitted call if there is at least one said admitted call.

20. The apparatus of claim 19 wherein in response to disestablishing a call previously admitted to said node, said user information table retrieves said parameters .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e of the disestablished call, and said accumulator reduces .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T by the retrieved values of .gamma. and .PSI..sub.e.

21. The apparatus of claim 16 wherein said node admits both type-1 traffic calls for streamed communications and type-2 traffic calls for transactional communications, said apparatus further comprising:

(d) a register maintaining an aggregate cell arrival rate parameter .GAMMA..sub.T of all type-2 traffic calls admitted to said node and said parameters .gamma..sub.T and .PSI..sub.T for all type-1 traffic calls admitted to said node,
wherein said power spectrum based admission control table uses .GAMMA..sub.T as well as the sum of .gamma..sub.T and .gamma. to retrieve .PSI..sub.max for determining whether or not to admit a type-1 traffic call.

22. The apparatus of claim 21 wherein in admitting a new type-2 traffic call, said first circuit produces a cell arrival rate .GAMMA..sub.new for said admitted type-2 traffic call, said predetermined power spectrum based connection admission control table retrieves a value of .PSI..sub.max contained in an entry indexed by the sum of .GAMMA..sub.T and .GAMMA..sub.new and .gamma..sub.T, and produces an indication indicating whether or not to admit said new type-2 traffic call by comparing a sum of .GAMMA..sub.T and .GAMMA..sub.new to a maximum aggregate cell arrival rate for type-2 traffic calls .GAMMA..sub.max and by comparing .PSI..sub.T to the value of .PSI..sub.max.
