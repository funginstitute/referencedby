Ink cartridge

Patent number: 6666551
Filing date: Nov 27, 2001
Issue date: Dec 23, 2003
Application number: 09/996,860

An ink cartridge may have an ink accommodating portion shaped to contain ink, an ink supply hole disposed at a bottom of the ink accommodating portion, and a valve unit including a movable valve portion and a fixed valve portion, the movable valve portion being oriented such that a flow of ink is regulated by a horizontal motion of the movable valve portion. The valve unit may further include a main portion and an auxiliary portion, the movable valve portion being disposed on the main portion and the fixed valve portion being disposed on the auxiliary portion, wherein the auxiliary portion is selectively detachable from the main portion. The ink cartridge may further comprise a supply hole attachment located at the bottom of the ink accommodating portion, the supply hole being defined in a selectively detachable supply hole attachment.

Claims

1. An ink cartridge for detachable mounting on a connecting member of an ink jet recording apparatus, the connecting member reciprocating in a horizontal plane when the ink jet recording apparatus prints, said ink cartridge comprising:

an ink accommodating portion shaped to contain ink;
an ink supply hole disposed at a bottom of said ink accommodating portion; and
a valve unit connected to the ink supply hole by a connecting portion and including a movable valve portion and a fixed valve portion, said movable valve portion being oriented such that a flow of ink is regulated by motion of said movable valve portion along a line lying in a plane that is parallel to the horizontal plane.

2. The ink cartridge of claim 1, the valve unit further including a main portion and an auxiliary portion, said movable valve portion being disposed on said main portion and said fixed valve portion being disposed on said auxiliary portion, wherein said auxiliary portion is selectively detachable from said main portion.

3. The ink cartridge of claim 1, further comprising:

a supply hole attachment disposed at said bottom of said ink accommodating portion, said supply hole being defined in said supply hole attachment, wherein said supply hole attachment is selectively detachable from said bottom of said ink accommodating portion.

4. The ink cartridge of claim 3, further comprising a packing member disposed in said supply hole attachment.

5. The ink cartridge of claim 3, further comprising a seal member dimensioned and disposed on said supply hole attachment to block said supply hole.

6. The ink cartridge of claim 1, further comprising a packing member disposed in said ink supply hole.

7. The ink cartridge of claim 1, further comprising a porous member disposed in said ink accommodating portion.

8. The ink cartridge of claim 1, wherein said ink supply hole lies in a horizontal plane.

9. An ink cartridge for detachable mounting on a connecting member of an ink jet recording apparatus, said ink cartridge comprising:

an ink accommodating portion shaped to contain ink, said ink accommodating portion including a frame member having a first open end and a second open end, an upper cover member, and a lower cover member, wherein said lower cover member is detachably disposed at said first open end and said upper cover member is detachably disposed at said second open end;
an ink supply hole disposed at a bottom of said ink accommodating portion; and
a valve unit connected to the ink supply hole by a connecting portion and including a movable valve portion and a fixed valve portion, said movable valve portion being oriented such that a flow of ink is regulated by a horizontal motion of said movable valve portion.

10. The ink cartridge of claim 9, wherein the frame member has a plurality of engaging projections, and further comprising:

a plurality of lower cover engaging legs disposed on said lower cover;
wherein said upper cover engaging legs and said lower cover engaging legs are shaped to engage the plurality of engaging projections.

said supply hole attachment being detachably fitted to said ink accommodating portion, said supply hole attachment having an inner portion comprising a resilient material dimensioned and disposed so that said supply hole attachment couples to the ink leading out tool in an ink-tight manner.
