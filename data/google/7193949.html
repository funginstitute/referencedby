A method is provided for recording data in a write-once type optical recording medium which can record data by forming a recording mark having a desired length and reduce jitter of a reproduced signal. The method comprises determining a pulse train pattern so that a level of a pulse is switched from the level corresponding to the level of the recording power Pw to the level corresponding to the level of the bottom power Pb in accordance with the length of a blank region to be formed immediately after formation of a first recording mark and the length of a second recording mark formed subsequent to the formation of the first recording mark.
