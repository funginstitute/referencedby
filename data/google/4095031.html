Claims

1. A thermoplastic resin body consisting essentially of a thermoplastic copolymer of ethylene and about 6 to 60% by weight of a polar vinyl monomer selected from the class consisting of

(a) vinyl acetate;
(b) ethyl acrylate;
(c) methyl acrylate;
(d) butyl acrylate; and
(e) acrylic acid and about 1 to 30% by weight of perfume oil.

2. The thermoplastic resin body of claim 1 wherein the thermoplastic copolymer is comprised of ethylene and about 6 to 18% by weight ethyl acrylate.

3. The thermoplastic resin body of claim 1 wherein the thermoplastic copolymer is comprised of ethylene and about 9 to 60% by weight of vinyl acetate.
