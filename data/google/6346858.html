a structure and method for improving differential amplifier operation is provided. High performance, wide bandwidth or very fast CMOS amplifiers are possible using the new circuit topology of the present invention. The differential amplifier of he present invention employs a novel common mode feedback circuit to back bias the body regions of the amplifying transistors in the differential amplifier. The novel configuration is achieved entirely using CMOS fabrication techniques and delivers high performance in both amplifier gain (G) and frequency response (fT) characteristics using a 1 micron (1) CMOS technology.

Claims

1. A method for forming a signal processing circuit, comprising:

forming a metal oxide semiconductor digital logic circuit using CMOS technology; and
concurrently fabricating a metal oxide semiconductor analog differential amplifier circuit using CMOS technology, wherein fabricating the analog differential amplifier circuit comprises:
fabricating a pair of amplifier circuits on a first portion of a substrate;
fabricating a pair of load devices on the first portion of the substrate, coupled to the pair of amplifier circuits; and
fabricating a common mode feedback circuit on a second portion of the substrate, the common mode feedback circuit being coupled to the pair of amplifier circuits.

2. The method of claim 1, wherein fabricating a metal oxide semiconductor analog differential amplifier circuit includes fabricating a metal oxide semiconductor analog differential amplifier which operates with a circuit power supply voltage of less than 1.0 Volt.

3. A method for forming a signal processing circuit, comprising:

forming a metal oxide semiconductor digital logic circuit using CMOS technology; and
concurrently fabricating a metal oxide semiconductor analog differential amplifier circuit using CMOS technology, wherein fabricating the analog differential amplifier circuit comprises:
fabricating a pair of amplifier circuits on a first portion of a substrate;
fabricating a pair of load devices on the first portion of the substrate, coupled to the pair of amplifier circuits, wherein the pair of amplifier circuits and the pair of load devices are stacked as circuit layers; and
fabricating a common mode feedback circuit on a second portion of the substrate, the common mode feedback circuit being coupled to the pair of amplifier circuits.

4. The method of claim 3, wherein forming a metal oxide semiconductor digital logic circuit and concurrently fabricating a metal oxide semiconductor analog differential amplifier circuit includes forming the digital logic circuit and the analog differential amplifier circuit on a single semiconducting wafer.

5. The method of claim 3, wherein fabricating the differential amplifying circuit includes fabricating a differential amplifying circuit capable of operating at frequencies in the gigahertz (MHz) range.

6. The method of claim 3 wherein fabricating the analog differential amplifier circuit includes forming the analog differential amplifier circuit to provide a common mode gain (ACM) of less than 3105.

7. A method for forming a signal processing circuit, comprising:

fabricating a common mode feedback circuit on a second portion of the substrate; and
