Ingestibles possessing intrinsic color change

 Hans O. Ribi

Patent number: 6607744
Filing date: Jun 23, 2000
Issue date: Aug 19, 2003
Application number: 09/602,001

Methods and compositions are provided comprising ingestibles and a thermochromic composition which is informative as to the temperature history of the ingestible, either prior or contemporaneous with use. Various solid or liquid ingestible compositions are provided for determining storage temperature, temperature of the user, temperature of the ingestible, particularly comestibles, and the like. Of particular interest are polydiacetylene polymers which may be formulated to provide compositions having transition temperature over a broad temperature range.

Claims

1. An ingestible comprising:

a polydiacetylene homopolymer having a transition termperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible, and wherein said polymer is triggered to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature by other than a binding pair interactions whereby the color of said ingestible is changed.

2. The ingestible according to claim 1, wherein said ingestible is a solid.

3. The ingestible according to claim 1, wherein said ingestible is a liquid.

4. A liquid ingestible comprising:

a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C. dispersed therein, wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible, and wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said ingestible is changed.

5. A solid ingestible having a coating comprising:

a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible and wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said coating is changed.

6. A solid ingestible according to claim 5, wherein said polymer interpenetrates said coating.

7. A composition comprising:

a carbohydrate interpenetrated with a physiologically acceptable polydiacetylene polymer other than a liposome having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said composition, and wherein said polymer changes color from a fist color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said carbohydrate is changed.

8. A composition comprising:

a lipid interpenetrated with a polydiacetylene polymer other than a liposome having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said composition, and wherein said polymer changes color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature in response to a trigger other than a binding pair interaction, whereby the color of said lipid is changed.

9. A solid ingestible comprising:

a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C. and at least one food dye, wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, wherein the combination of said polymer and said at least one food dye imparts a color different from said first color and said second color to said ingestible, and wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible.

10. A sugar icing comprising:

a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said sugar icing, and wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said sugar icing is changed.

11. A lipid ingestible coating composition comprising:

a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said lipid ingestible coating composition, wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said coating is changed.

12. An ingestible having a interpenetrating coating of a polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said ingestible is selected from the group consisting of medicaments, meats, confections, candy, baby food, cereals, marshmallows, cheese and baked goods, wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said ingestible is changed.

13. A liquid ingestible having dispersed therein a polydiacetylene homopolymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C. selected from the group consisting of hot and cold beverages, formulas and syrups, and wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, whereby the color of said ingestible is changed.

14. A solid food having an adherent edible coating comprising:

a design formed by a polydiacetylenic polymer, wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and said adherent coating can be consumed with said solid food, and wherein said polymer is triggered by other than a biochemical interaction to change color from a first color to a second color at a transition temperature, whereby said design changes color.

15. The solid food according to claim 14, wherein said coating comprises rice paper or sugar layers.

16. A solid material in contact with food, said solid material comprising a physiologically acceptable polydiacetylene homopolymer that undergoes a color change from a first color to a second color at a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., whereby the color of said solid material is changed, wherein said color change of said polymer is reversible, and wherein the trigger for said change is other than biochemical.

17. The solid material according to claim 16, wherein said material is a packaging material.

18. An ingestible comprising:

a polydiacetylene homopolymer, wherein said polydiacetylenic polymer comprises as an end group a carboxylic acid or derivative thereof, an hydroxy group or ether thereof, an amino group or derivative thereof, wherein said polymer interpenetrates said ingestible and wherein said polydiacetylene homopolymer changes color from a first color to a second color in response to a trigger other than a binding pair interaction, whereby the color of said ingestible is changed.

19. An ingestible comprising:

a polydiacetylene homopolymer, wherein said polydiacetylenic polymer is other than a liposome and comprises as an end group a carboxylic acid or derivative thereof, an hydroxy group or either thereof, an amino group or derivative thereof, wherein said polymer interpenetrates said ingestible, wherein said polydiacetylenic homopolymer changes color from a first color to a second color in response to a trigger and wherein said trigger is selected from the group consisting of ionic strength change, mechanical stress, chemical change, hydration state, enzymatic change, temperature change, pH change, radiation exposure, solvent change and physical stress.

20. The ingestible according to claim 19, wherein said temperature change is a decrease in said temperature.

21. An ingestible comprising:

an interpenetrating coating of a physiologically acceptable polydiacetylene polymer having a transition temperature in the range of about 10 to 200 C., wherein said ingestible is a condiment, and wherein said polymer changes color from a first color to a second color at said transition temperature, and wherein the trigger is other than a binding pair interaction, whereby the color of said condiment is changed.

22. The ingestible according to claim 21, wherein said condiment is selected from the group consisting of a salt and a sugar.

23. An ingestible composition comprising:

at least one of a monomeric and a polymeric diacetylenic compound other than a liposome, wherein said diacetylenic compound is physiologically acceptable, and wherein said diacetylenic compound can polymerize to form or is a polydiacetylene that changes color from a first color to a second color in response to a trigger selected from the group consisting of temperature change, pH change, radiation, ionic strength change, mechanical stress, chemical change, solvent change, hydration state, and enzymatic change; and
at least one ingestible carrier material, wherein said ingestible carrier material is selected from the group consisting of an aqueous solution, a solvent-based solution, an oil, a lipid, a sugar, a salt, a seasoning, a lectin, an agglutinin, a protein matrix, and a carbohydrate matrix, and wherein said composition is formed by interpenetrating said diacetylenic compound with said carrier material.

24. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said solvent is ethanol.

25. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said at least one ingestible carrier material is a laminate of a sugar or a salt alone or in combination with a seasoning.

26. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said interpenetrating of said diacetylenic compound with said carrier material is by a method selected from the group consisting of ink jet printing, pad printing, extruding, spraying, liquid applicators, dip coating, sublimating, and spreading.

27. The ingestible composition according to claim 26, wherein said interpenetrating is in a form selected from the group consisting of a message, a pattern, a design, a logo, and a bar code.

28. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said composition is an ancillary material that can be applied to a food product and said trigger is hydration.

29. The ingestible composition according to claim 28, wherein said ancillary material is selected from the group consisting of carbohydrates, granulated sugar, sugar sprinkles, fondant, sugar pastes, candies, nutritional bits, food coatings, condiments, carriers, emulsifiers, and coating materials.

30. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein prior to exposure to said trigger, said composition is a natural food color.

31. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said trigger is selected from the group consisting of mechanical pressure, light exposure and enzymatic change.

32. An ingestible composition comprising:

a polydiacetylene homopolymer other than a liposome that in response to a change in a trigger selected from the group consisting of temperature change, pH change, radiation, ionic strength change, mechanical stress, chemical change, solvent change, hydration state, and enzymatic change can transition between an ordered first colored structure and a disordered second colored structure, wherein the colors of said first colored structure and said second colored structure are different and wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible.

33. The ingestible composition according to claim 32, wherein said first colored structure is blue and said second colored structure is red.

34. The ingestible composition according to claim 32, wherein said trigger is a temperature change.

35. The ingestible composition according to claim 32, wherein said transition is reversible.

36. The ingestible according to any one of claims 12, 13, 18, 21, or 22 wherein said polymer is physiologically acceptable and can be consumed with said ingestible.

37. The ingestible according to claim 3, wherein said liquid is an alcohol.

38. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said monomeric diacetylenic compound is colorless.

39. The ingestible composition according to claim 23, wherein said monomer comprises at least 1% of said ingestible.
